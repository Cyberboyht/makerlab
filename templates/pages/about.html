What is a makerlab ?

The MakerSpaces serve as a meeting point where communities of Makers (creators) connect to work on real and personally meaningful projects, advised by helpful experienced mentors, making use of new technologies and traditional tools. They can be found in different shapes and sizes, but all of them are characterized by what they allow us to do.
